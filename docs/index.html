Story of StatWizard

Ever been curious about the magic behind data? I sure was. It's like deciphering a secret language, and that's where my journey began. I found this enchanting world of statistics, machine learning and new data science technologies - fascinating, but hidden behind esoteric academic journals and multiple layers of paywalls.

So, I decided to create StatWizard, a sort of digital Hogwarts for regular folks like you and me. Think of StatWizard as your guide to becoming a wizard of statistics. I simply want to build a community where we all learn together irrespective of our diverse backgrounds, and this website is the first step where I share whatever interesting stuffs I learn when experimenting and working with data on a day-to-day basis, using open source tools.

StatWizard will always be a free haven for those who wants to master the wizardry of statistics, without any ads or any paywalls. However, you can always support the cause by tossing a few coins to cover up the hosting costs, or reaching out to me with an idea of writing any interesting content.
